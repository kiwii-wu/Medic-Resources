Medical Student Resources

This is a compilation of helpful resources for medical students studying for medical finals. We will highlight tried-and-tested resources for studying medicine. The site will be expanded to incorporate resources covering different specialties within the medical school curriculum.

Shotgun Pathology

From the youtube channel WashingtonDeceit, these histology lectures are concise, well-explained and clincally relevant.

Sam Webster

Sam Webster is an anatomy lecturer in Wales. He makes good use of the anatomy models to explain concepts in a visual way. His descriptions are accurate and helps you curate a map of the human body in your mind.

Zero to Finals

Zero to Finals offers concise, clinically relevant explanations and notes on a large proportion of the clincial curriculum. High yield and bound to help you score points on your MCQ exams.
